NoteHub is a note-sharing service meant for users of all ages. It is a school project kindly brought to you by four Hwa Chong Institution schoolboys.

NoteHub is a school project (Category 9, Infocomm) which aims to bring some good into the world by launching a secure, safe, reliable platform to share, edit and view notes.

We are constantly editing and devising methods to make your user experience much more comfortable. If you have any queries, please contact us at the.notehub@gmail.com.
