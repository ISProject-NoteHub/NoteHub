Project Title NoteHub - The Free Notesharing Service
Synopsis NoteHub is a note-sharing service for anyone and everyone, mainly students which makes finding the notes they need online, which would have otherwise been fragmented across the Internet, easier. It creates a wide gallery of notes which users can browse through by subject at their own leisure.

One of the key features of NoteHub is ease of collaboration between different users on the Internet. The creator of a note can invite collaborators to work on the note he or she has created, which allows the collaborators to edit the note.

As all notes on NoteHub are automatically made public, the users can read notes that others have published and make suggestions to further improve the note. However, only the collaborators of that particular note can edit the note.
