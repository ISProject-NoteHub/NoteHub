Free Notes... For All!

NoteHub is a free notesharing services for anyone and everyone - you may be a student, a businessman or the owner of a start-up if you wish - as long as you need notes, you seek to benefit from NoteHub.

You can help other people too - NoteHub's a notesharing service, so your notes can be easily shared with everyone - for people to see, for people to explore in a gallery, and for people to improve on them.

Image courtesy https://www.incimages.com/.

Harness the Power of Collaboration

Whilst people seek to benefit from your notes, you can benefit too! Anyone can add suggestions, comments or improvements to your notes, and these can help you know how, and where, you can improve - indeed, everyone wins!

Of course, we do offer standard direct collaboration where collaborators can edit your notes directly as well (even if they don't have an account)!
