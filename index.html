About

This is our Independent Studies Infocomm project.

In the space of 8 months, we are going to come up with an idea and execute it.

The end product should be a finished, working product which will be useful for either the school community, the country or the world.

Programmer's Logbook

2 Feb 2017

We have drafted out 4 ideas for our project, listed below:

  1. FinicKEY - 3D escape locked room/point-and-click game
  2. NoteHub - Note sharing service
  3. CodeAwesome (Mobile Version) - Brings programming to smartphones
  4. Hwa Chong Utilities - Combines attendance taking app, i Electronic Message Board (iEMB) and other helpful apps into one

For more information, check our proposal lists through this link: https://docs.google.com/a/hci.edu.sg/document/d/13vBDP9AG4WRdmlA2soKA5gzX1OyuFeF23wS73eaS5mA/edit?usp=sharing
