Free Notes... For All!

NoteHub is a free notesharing service open to all. No matter who you are - you may be a student, a businessman or the owner of a start-up - as long as you need notes, you seek to benefit from NoteHub.

Help others and grow in knowledge together! You can help other people too - NoteHub's a notesharing service, so your notes can be easily shared with everyone - for people to see, for people to explore in a gallery, and for people to improve on them. After all, sharing is (ahem) caring. Be a pal and lend a hand to those in need!

Harness the Power of Collaboration

Whilst people seek to benefit from your notes, you can benefit from theirs too! NoteHub provides a big worldwide community of note takers where anyone can add suggestions, comments or improvements to your notes. Improve from your mistakes and hep others realise theirs to create notes of better quality for everyone - indeed, everyone wins!

Of course, we do offer standard direct collaboration where collaborators can edit your notes directly as well (even if they don't have an account)!
