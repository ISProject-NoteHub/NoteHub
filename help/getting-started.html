Getting Started with NoteHub

NoteHub is a service centered around taking and sharing notes.

To take a note, click on the 'New Note' button on the front page. There, you can take your notes to your heart's content -

WAIT.

If you don't save your work, all of your precious information will be lost!

To save your work, simply click the 'Save' button on the bottom bar to save it to your account. If you don't have an account yet, create one here.

Sharing your work with others is easy! Simply click the 'Invite Collaborators' button at the top of the page and invite your friends, classmates and colleagues using their IP addresses or their NoteHub account.

Only collaborators can directly edit your note. Other people can only suggest.

Gallery

The gallery is a place where everyone's notes can be seen and commented on.

It is specially curated for each individual user by our intelligent algorithms. (However, the algorithm is fallible, so please send feedback to us via our email (the.notehub@gmail.com) if you find anything wrong with NoteHub).
